Bachelier Maths

Overview

Bachelier Maths is a practice-focused revision app for French high school students preparing for the baccalauréat. It emphasizes adaptive training, daily targeted practice, spaced repetition, and clear mastery tracking.

Key Features

  • Adaptive training focused on the student’s weak areas
  • Questions modeled on actual baccalauréat exams
  • Spaced repetition to reinforce learning over time
  • Clear visual feedback: green, yellow, red mastery rings
  • Content created by a professeur agrégé with deep experience in baccalauréat preparation

Overview

For le baccalauréat

Bachelier Maths is a practice-first revision app designed specifically for French high-school students preparing for the baccalauréat. The app focuses on adaptive training to strengthen weak areas, exam-mode questions modelled on real papers, and spaced repetition to keep knowledge long-term.

Key Features

  • Adaptive training prioritising weak topics
  • Questions modelled on recent baccalauréat exams
  • Spaced repetition schedules to reinforce memory
  • Clear mastery rings (green / yellow / red) for immediate feedback
  • Content created by a professeur agrégé, exam-focused
  • Daily targets & progress notifications
